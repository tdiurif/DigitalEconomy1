TSUE Alumni

On August 13, 1931, the Central Asian Institute of Finance and Economics was founded on the foundation of Turkestan State University’s Faculty of Economics and Social Sciences, based on the locations of the former Council of People’s Commissars of the Union. This was done in response to the growing demand for economists. The Tashkent Institute of Finance and Economics was established as a result to train personnel for the Central Asian countries’ financial and credit systems.In Uzbekistan, this was the country’s inaugural economic training phase. The first bell at the institute rung on September 1, 1931, signaling the start of classes.

Islom Karimov

The first president of the Republic of Uzbekistan

State and political figure, the first president of the Republic of Uzbekistan. Hero Of Uzbekistan (1994). Supreme Commander-in-chief of the Armed Forces of Uzbekistan and winner of the presidential elections of 2007, winner of the presidential elections of 2015. The first secretary of the Communist Party of Uzbekistan until the declaration of independence. President of Uzbekistan from 1991 to the end of his life. Under the leadership of Islam Karimov, Uzbekistan had its own constitution, was recognized in the UN and had equal relations with the CIS countries.
